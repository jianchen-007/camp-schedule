🏕️ Week in Preview

Summer 2026

Good to know

Activities marked with * require advance sign-up in the sign-up notebook (they're the shaded ones on the printed sheet). This app just tracks what you personally plan to do — it doesn't sign you up with the camp office.

Office hours: Saturday 8–10am and 3–10pm; Sunday–Friday 8am–noon and 1–10pm.

Fountain & Boat Dock hours: check the Guest Display board by the Office.

Ski Dock sign-ups take place at the Ski Dock every day of the week.

Schedules update daily — each evening's new "Whatzappening?!" details are added to the app. To load the latest info, tap the 🔄 button at the top of the screen.

Days and times of events are subject to change — check the Whatzappening board for the latest.

Official camp website: 🌐 whatzappening.com

Your checked-off activities are saved only on this device (offline, no account/server). Use "Export" in My Sign-Ups to back them up as text.
